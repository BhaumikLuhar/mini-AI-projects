# Document Review

## Summary

The Information Security Policy defines mandatory security practices for employees and contractors, with strict controls on access, monitoring, data retention, and compliance enforcement.

- Policy mandates multi-factor authentication for users.
- Customer data must be retained for seven years, raising potential privacy concerns.
